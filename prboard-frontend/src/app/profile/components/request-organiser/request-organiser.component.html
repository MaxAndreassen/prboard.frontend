Affiliate Application

In order to host events on ECompete as an affiliate, we first need to do a quick review of your account and the sorts of events you'd like to run. This is usually a very quick process.

Once your application is accepted you will be able to run events through the platform and also earn by referring others to the platform using your personalised affiliate link.

By submitting this application you agree to be bound by the terms of our affiliate agreement.

We use this to ensure we don't cross promote your affiliated users to direct competitors as specified in our affiliate agreement.

We will now review your request as soon as we can, once we do this we will upgrade your account to an event organiser and you will be able to host your own events.
